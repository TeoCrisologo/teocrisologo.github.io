This page contains a variety of charts - some more interactive than others - that I have been building as part of the course PP 434: Automated Data Visualization for Public Policy. On this homepage I present my portfolio with coding challenges divided by each week. In the link below you can find access to my final project of the course related to labour informality in Peru.

In this first task, we have included two charts from the Richard Davies Library.
